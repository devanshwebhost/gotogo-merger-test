Answer & Questions

What is RoomsnVenues?

RoomsnVenues.com is an online and offline booking portal with wide range of rooms and venues available in Delhi NCR, Gurgaon, Noida, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad along with International holiday packages. At RoomsnVenues, we connect our customers with the best rooms and venues in their city based on their unique requirements so that they can get the ideal deal for their event. We believe that online room and venue booking is just as personal as offline venue booking, except much simpler and our goal is to provide hassle-free online venue booking experience to our customers, all the way from browsing and shortlisting venues to booking the final one.

An upcoming event:How can RoomsnVenues help?

RoomsnVenues helps you plan your event without hassles by letting you explore your room and/or venue options based on your unique requirements and connecting you to the right Hotel/venue making the search convenient for you. We help you with plan your event with three things:
● Find rooms/venues/packages you may not know of, based on your requirements.
● Get availability of multiple shortlisted rooms/venues/packages in one go without you having to call and visit all venue options to confirm availability and receive package details.
● Get negotiated prices for your shortlisted venues/vendors and enable you to review and compare each shortlisted venue, helping you make an informed and right decision.

Are you an even planner?

No, we do not plan events. Instead, we empower you to plan your event hassle-free through the use of technology, comprehensive and current information of rooms/ venues and packages.
