GoToGo Travel Solutions – Safety Protocols

Safety is the foundation across all GoToGo services.

1. Chauffeur Safety Standards

(Fleet, Shuttle, Airport Shuttle)

  • Background-verified drivers
  • Defensive driving training
  • Hospitality etiquette training
  • GPS-enabled route tracking

2. Vehicle Safety & Hygiene

  • Regular sanitization after every ride
  • Daily mechanical checks
  • Mandatory seatbelts
  • AC & comfort checks before dispatch

3. Passenger Safety During Shuttle & Tours

  • GPS tracking shared with backend team
  • 24/7 support line
  • SOS protocols for emergencies
  • Trained drivers for long-distance trips (Agra Shuttle)

4. RoomsnVenues Safety Standards

We partner only with verified hotels & venues that follow:

  • Fire & safety compliance
  • Guest security protocols
  • Cleanliness & hygiene standards

5. Digital & Payment Safety

  • Encrypted payments
  • Secure OTP verification
  • No unauthorized data sharing

6. Corporate & International Traveler Safety

  • Verified chauffeurs
  • Multi-language support on request
  • Clear communication about itinerary & timings
  • Punctual airport transfers to avoid missed flights
